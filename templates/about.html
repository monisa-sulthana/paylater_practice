About

Paylater is a digital financial services platform that specializes in consumer lending. They empower individuals with access to credit, simple payments solutions,
high-yield investment opportunities and easy-to-use tools for personal financial management.With PayLater, customers can purchase small consumer
durable or other household items in a completely digital and paperless manner. This facility is an instant digital credit limit that helps customers to shop online, pay bills,
and pay using the Merchant UPI ID at physical stores.
